About Me

Who I Am

I am a Data Analyst with a passion for discovering insightful information in data and transforming it into practical business solutions. I specialize in SQL, Power BI, DAX, and M language, with experience in data analysis across various industries.

My journey in the world of data began with a fascination with the possibilities that data analysis offers in making better business decisions. Since then, I have been developing my skills to deliver valuable insights and solutions.

Experience and Education

I have experience in analytical projects involving data exploration, data cleaning, modeling, and visualization using tools such as SQL, Power BI, and Python.

I continuously develop my skills through online courses, certificates, and practical projects that you can see in my portfolio.

My Approach

I believe that effective data analysis is more than just numbers - it's telling stories through data. My approach includes:

  1. Understanding Business Context - I start each project by thoroughly understanding the business objectives and questions that the data needs to answer.
  2. Thorough Analysis - I care about the quality and accuracy of data, applying rigorous cleaning and validation methods.
  3. Practical Insights - I focus on transforming data into practical insights that can lead to specific actions and decisions.
  4. Effective Communication - I communicate results in a clear and understandable way, using visualizations to present complex information.
