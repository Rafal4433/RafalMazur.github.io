About Me

Professional Journey

I am a BI Developer with a passion for discovering insightful information in data and transforming it into practical business solutions. With experience in analytical projects involving data exploration, cleaning, modeling, and visualization, I specialize in SQL, Python and Power BI across various industries.

My journey in the world of data began with a fascination with the possibilities that data analysis offers in making better business decisions. Since then, I have been developing my skills to deliver valuable insights and solutions. I continuously expand my knowledge through online courses, certifications, and practical projects that you can explore in my portfolio.

My Approach

I believe that effective data analysis is more than just numbers - it's telling stories through data. My approach includes:

  1. Understanding Business Context - I start each project by thoroughly understanding the business objectives and questions that the data needs to answer.
  2. Thorough Analysis - I care about the quality and accuracy of data, applying rigorous cleaning and validation methods.
  3. Practical Insights - I focus on transforming data into practical insights that can lead to specific actions and decisions.
  4. Effective Communication - I communicate results in a clear and understandable way, using visualizations to present complex information.
